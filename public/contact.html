Quick Answers

Got questions? We've got you covered. Here are the most common things folks ask about working with us.

What kind of projects do you take on?

We focus on small-to-mid-sized indie games—think narrative adventures, experimental mechanics, retro-inspired platformers. If it's weird, heartfelt, or pushing boundaries, we're interested. We're not the right fit for AAA-scale or mobile gacha stuff.

Can I playtest your games early?

Absolutely! We love getting feedback from real players. Sign up for our newsletter or follow us on social media—we announce playtest opportunities there first. Sometimes we'll reach out directly if you've been active in the community.

Are your games available for streaming or press coverage?

Yes! We're thrilled when people stream, review, or write about our work. No need to ask permission—just go for it. If you want early access keys or assets for coverage, drop us a line and we'll hook you up.

How long does a typical collaboration take?

Depends on scope, but most projects run 3-6 months from concept to launch. We're indie, so timelines are flexible—we'd rather ship something great than rush something half-baked. We'll keep you posted every step of the way.

What makes a project a good fit for SJohnson?

If you value creativity over polish, story over graphics, and you're okay with a little chaos in the dev process—we're your team. We thrive on projects that let us experiment, break rules, and make something memorable. Bonus points if you love arcade nostalgia.
